Reclaim Your Good Luck Back

1. Place Rosemary (fresh or dried) in your left hand.

2. Add a bit of your perfume.

3. Add some salt and a few drops of lemon.

4. Rub your hands together while saying:

"By the power of this Rosemary, I remove all obstacles in my path.
By the power of this lemon, I cut all negativity in my life.
And by the power of this salt, all my luck shall return to me. Àṣẹ."

After the 3rd time say: "So I speak and so shall it be. And so it is...

5. Rinse your hands thoroughly with water and let them air dry.

6. Repeat the ritual two to three times a week until you feel your luck has improved.
